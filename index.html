About Us

I am Harvinder Mathur, a Stock Trading Coach, Business Consultant, and Financial Educator dedicated to simplifying wealth creation for learners of all ages. A graduate of Delhi University, I have a diverse professional background spanning hospitality, entrepreneurship, and financial markets, which allows me to combine practical business insight with real-world experience in every session I conduct.

Over the years, I have worked with renowned hospitality brands, built extensive distribution networks, and now guide individuals and organizations in mastering the art of intelligent investing. Each step of this journey has strengthened my belief that true financial empowerment comes from knowledge, clarity, and disciplined action.

As we often say in our sessions — our teaching philosophy is simple yet effective:

Making money in the stock market is as easy as making chapati in the kitchen.– But one should know the PROCESS
